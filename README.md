# Decision Quality Lab

A lightweight, open-source decision quality review tool for leaders, product teams, and transformation professionals.

Decision Quality Lab helps users structure an important decision, identify missing information, examine risks, compare alternatives, clarify ownership, and assess governance readiness before commitment.

## Live Demo

[Open Decision Quality Lab](https://hebahamour-code.github.io/decision-quality-lab/)

## Why This Project

Important decisions often move forward before their assumptions, risks, alternatives, and ownership are clearly examined.

This prototype demonstrates how structured decision review can improve decision quality before execution.

## Current Features

- Progressive scoring across seven decision-quality dimensions
- Per-dimension score breakdown
- Priority improvement recommendations
- Structured decision review form
- Decision quality score
- Decision readiness assessment
- Evidence review
- Risk awareness check
- Alternative analysis
- Stakeholder impact review
- Decision ownership check
- Governance recommendations
- Responsive mobile-friendly interface
- Client-side processing with no data storage

## How the Score Works
## Scoring Model

Each review dimension receives a progressive score from 0 to 100 based on the completeness and relevance of the information provided.

The final score is calculated using weighted dimensions:

- Decision definition — 20%
- Desired outcome — 15%
- Evidence quality — 20%
- Risk awareness — 15%
- Alternative analysis — 15%
- Stakeholder impact — 10%
- Decision ownership — 5%

The score measures structural completeness, not whether the decision itself is correct.

The current version uses transparent rule-based scoring across seven dimensions:

1. Decision definition
2. Desired outcome
3. Evidence quality
4. Risk awareness
5. Alternative analysis
6. Stakeholder impact
7. Decision ownership

The tool does not currently use artificial intelligence.

## Privacy

All analysis currently runs inside the user’s browser.

No information is transmitted, stored, or shared.

Users should still avoid entering confidential, regulated, or personally identifiable information.

## Disclaimer

Decision Quality Lab is an experimental decision-support prototype.

It does not provide legal, financial, compliance, or professional advice. It supports human judgment and does not replace accountable decision-making.
